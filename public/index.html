This is my attempt to set up a website where my cousin and I can play toroidal chess over the internet. This app is currently in its primitive stages. When you connect, you will be put in a room. If there is another player in the room, you can begin playing immediately. If not, you must wait for another player to join the room before you can start making moves. In the future I might make this more sophisticated, such as adding support for choosing your own room, nicknames, chat, etc. The rules for toroidal chess can be found here.
